# Brand & Vision

## Positioning
Science-driven mental fitness at the intersection of neuroscience, psychology, and performance coaching.

## Target Audience [CONFIRMED]
- High-performing professionals in high-stress roles
- Already invest in coaching, bio-tracking, productivity tools
- Suffering from: burnout, brain fog, energy management, ADHD symptoms
- Audience overlap: Huberman, Tim Ferriss, Whoop, DOAC, Joe Dispenza

## Brand Voice [CONFIRMED]
- **Tone:** Evidence-based but inspiring — Stanford lab-notes × Patagonia campaign
- **Personality:** Guide-Inventor — trusted educator who prototypes the future
- **Visuals:** Clean typographic hierarchy, cyan/teal accent, line-art brain-body diagrams

## Core Message
Adapt faster, stress less, thrive longer.

## Differentiators [CONFIRMED]
1. Scientific depth (cutting-edge literature → practical protocols)
2. Measurement (biomarkers + psychometrics + behavior telemetry)
3. Accreditation target (CPD/HCPC recognition)
4. Hybrid delivery (app + live + AI nudges)

## Operational Detail
- **Repo:** bodycog-marketing (brand guidelines, messaging docs)
- **Process defined?** Partial — brand voice documented, no systematic brand audit process
