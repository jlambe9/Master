## Model Inputs

### Pipeline A — Targeted CRs (ongoing engine)
- 35 CRs/day via Expandi (ADHD ICP, screened)
- Optimised rates: 52% accept, 20% intro reply
- Intro + FU1 only for first 29 days (while Pipeline B active)
- Full sequence after day 29
- 31 messages/day → 58 messages/day after day 29
- **Steady state: £7,250/mo**

### Pipeline B — Existing Contacts (one-shot boost)
- 5,500 connections → ICP filter + competitor/coach screen → ~1,750 leads
- 140 messages/day total limit, Pipeline A takes 31 → 109 for Pipeline B
- 61 intros/day, intro + FU1 only
- Lower rates (general ICP): 12% intro reply, 19.9% conversation, 20% call booking, 75% show, 15% close
- Exhausted in 29 days
- **Total lifetime yield: ~£5,200**

### Content Engine (amplifier + inbound channel)
- 3 posts/day from defined content calendar
- 5,500 starting followers
- Impressions compound: 500/post → 1,500 → 3,000 over 90 days

## Pipeline B vs Full Sequence Trade-Off

Intro+FU1 only (Scenario B) chosen over full sequence (Scenario A) because:
- 2x revenue per day from Pipeline B (£179/day vs £91/day)
- 29 days to exhaust vs 73 days
- Front-loads revenue (cash needed NOW)
- After day 29, Pipeline A switches to full sequence with spare capacity
- 90-day total nearly identical (~£200 difference)
