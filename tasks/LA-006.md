## Process

### Step 1: Notify the council (do on move-in day or before)
Go to: [Council Tax Moving In/Out Form](https://lbhf-self.achieveservice.com/service/Council_tax_moving_in_moving_out)
- Enter old address, new Fulham address, and move-in date
- Submit — they'll update records and send a bill

### Step 2: Create online account
Go to: [My Account Registration](https://lbhf-self.achieveservice.com/)
- Or via: https://www.lbhf.gov.uk/my-account/creating-account-and-logging
- Add your council tax account using your address or reference number from the bill

### Step 3: Set up Direct Debit
- Log into My Account
- Set up DD (standard is 10 monthly instalments April-January)
- Can also pay monthly over 12 months if preferred

### Step 4: Check your band & apply for discounts
- Check band: https://www.gov.uk/council-tax-bands (enter new address)
- If living alone: apply for 25% single occupier discount via My Account
- If living with Saida: no discount (2 adults)

## Key Info
- **Borough:** London Borough of Hammersmith & Fulham (LBHF)
- **Estimated amount:** ~£150/mo (budgeted) — actual depends on band
- **Council tax page:** https://www.lbhf.gov.uk/council-tax
- **Contact (if needed):** https://www.lbhf.gov.uk/council-tax/contact-us-about-council-tax (Mon-Fri 9-5)
- **Tax year:** April 2025 – March 2026

## Notes
- Do this ASAP after moving to avoid backdated charges
- If moving mid-month, you'll get a pro-rata bill for the first period
- Keep the council tax reference number — needed for electoral roll, parking permits etc
