> **Summary:** Calculate actual monthly living expenses including medication costs and new flat costs. This is the foundation — everything else (audit, plan, KPIs) depends on knowing this number.

## Win State

A single number: "Jamie needs £X/month to live." Broken down by category, including medication and new flat costs.

## What to Include

### Fixed Monthly
- Rent (new flat — what's the actual number?)
- Council tax
- Utilities (gas, electric, water, internet)
- Phone
- Insurance (any)
- ADHD medication (prescription costs, private vs NHS?)
- Other medication/supplements
- Gym membership

### Variable Monthly
- Food/groceries
- Transport (Oyster, Uber)
- Social/eating out
- Subscriptions (Revolut virtual cards — pull the list)
- Business tools (Claude Max, Expandi, PhantomBuster, Google AI Pro, GoHighLevel, domain/hosting)

### One-Off / Irregular
- Moving costs (deposit, agency fees, overlap rent)
- Medical bills (what's outstanding?)
- Double rent period (how long?)
