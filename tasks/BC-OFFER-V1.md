## Sections Required
1. **One-Sentence Pitch** — "I help [person] [result] through [mechanism] in [timeframe]"
2. **Problem Statement** — ICP, what they experience, what hasn't worked, cost of inaction
3. **Mechanism** — Jamie's unique angle: ADHD neuroscience × physical interventions × personalised protocol
4. **Deliverables** — Sessions, frequency, tools, between-session support, onboarding
5. **Transformation / Outcomes** — Before→After, measurable, timeline by week
6. **Pricing & Packaging** — £297-£497 for 6-8 weeks ⚠️ REVIEW, founding member framing
7. **Differentiation** — vs ADHD coaching, therapy, productivity coaching, wellness, meds alone
8. **Proof / Credibility** — Jamie's background, science, case studies, N=1
9. **Objection Handling** — "already take meds", "tried coaching", "no time", "vs Google"
10. **Sales Call Flow** — Opening → problem → mechanism → offer → proof → close
