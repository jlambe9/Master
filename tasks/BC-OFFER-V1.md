The outreach pipeline is sending CRs and writing intros — but what are we selling? Without a defined offer:

- Sales calls have no structure, deliverables, or pricing to present
- No landing page can be built

Everything downstream depends on this being defined.

## Sections Required

### 1. One-Sentence Pitch
What is it, who is it for, what result does it deliver — in one sentence.
- Must work in: LinkedIn DM, cold email, verbal intro, landing page headline
- Template: "I help [specific person] [achieve specific result] through [mechanism] in [timeframe]"

### 2. Problem Statement
- Who is this for? (specific ICP: ADHD founders/execs experiencing X)
- What are they experiencing? (reference `reference/problems-we-solve.md`)
- What have they tried that hasn't worked? (meds alone, productivity hacks, willpower)
- What's the cost of NOT solving this? (burnout, business failure, health decline)

### 3. Mechanism
- HOW does this actually work? What's the methodology?
- Jamie's unique angle: ADHD neuroscience × physical interventions × personalised protocol
- Key insight: ADHD is partly a metabolic/energy problem, not just a focus problem
- What makes this different from: ADHD coaching, therapy, productivity coaching, wellness coaching?
- Science-backed but accessible explanation

### 4. Deliverables (what they GET)
- Number of sessions, frequency, duration
- What happens in each session?
- Any tools, assessments, or materials included?
- Between-session support (WhatsApp? Telegram? Email?)
- Any tech/tracking component?
- Onboarding process

### 5. Transformation / Outcomes
- Before state → After state (concrete, not vague)
- Measurable outcomes where possible (energy levels, focus hours, habit consistency)
- Timeline: what changes by week 2, week 4, week 6-8?
- What does "success" look like for a typical client?

### 6. Pricing & Packaging
- V1 price point (previous thinking: £297-£497 for 6-8 weeks)
- What's included at each tier (if multiple)
- Payment terms
- Guarantee/risk reversal (if any)
- Founding member / early adopter framing?

### 7. Differentiation
- vs ADHD coaching: "We address the physical/metabolic root, not just behavioural strategies"
- vs therapy: "Not clinical — performance-focused, action-oriented"
- vs productivity coaching: "Addresses WHY your brain can't execute, not just giving you another system"
- vs wellness coaching: "Specific to ADHD neuroscience, not generic wellness"
- vs medication alone: "Meds are a lever, not a solution — this builds the foundation meds work on top of"

### 8. Proof / Credibility
- Jamie's background (what qualifies him?)
- Scientific basis (studies, mechanisms)
- Case studies (even N=1 — Jamie's own experience)
- Testimonials (when available — placeholder for now)
- Any beta results?

### 9. Objection Handling
- "I already take meds" → meds + this = compounding effect
- "I've tried coaching before" → this isn't traditional coaching
- "I don't have time" → the time cost of NOT fixing this
- "How is this different from what I can Google?" → personalisation + accountability + the metabolic angle
- "I'm not sure I have ADHD" → you don't need a diagnosis, the problems are real regardless

### 10. Sales Call Flow
- Opening (rapport, understand their situation)
- Problem exploration (what are they experiencing?)
- Mechanism reveal (here's why those problems exist)
- Offer presentation (here's what I do about it)
- Social proof
- Close
- (Reference BOSS-SALES for full call workflow)

## What's NOT in V1

- Polished landing page copy (comes after offer is solid)
- Course materials / session plans (comes after first 2-3 clients)
- Automated onboarding flow (manual is fine at 0-10 clients)
- Group offer (1:1 first, group later)
