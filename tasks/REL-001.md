# REL-001 — Write "Moving In Talk" with Saida

> **Summary:** Write out a clear, honest framework for the conversation about moving in together. Cover expectations, timelines, and the tension between Jamie's timeline pressure pain and Saida's need for security/commitment.

**Domain:** personal/relationship
**Priority:** 🟡 HIGH
**Status:** CAPTURED
**Type:** EXPLORE
**Cat:** 🔴 A-I (intrinsically hard — deep emotional territory)
**Created:** 2026-02-12

## Win State

A written document (for Jamie's eyes, not to read aloud) that clarifies his own thinking, so the conversation is honest, structured, and compassionate rather than reactive.

## What to Write Out

### 1. Both Sets of Expectations
- What does Jamie want from living together?
- What does Saida want?
- Where do these align? Where do they diverge?

### 2. Conversion / Commitment
- What does "moving in" mean to each of them? (Step towards marriage? Practical? Test run?)
- What's the implicit expectation around engagement/marriage timeline?
- Be honest about where Jamie is on this

### 3. Timelines — The Core Tension
**Jamie's reality:**
- Timeline pressure is causing a huge amount of pain and stress
- Financial uncertainty makes committing to dates feel impossible
- Pressure to commit to a timeline when the ground is shifting underneath

**Saida's reality:**
- Without a timeline, she feels insecure/unhappy
- Uncertainty about commitment is painful for her too
- She needs something to hold onto

**The question:** What can we do about this tension? Options:
- Conditional timelines ("If X happens by Y, then we move by Z")
- Process commitments instead of date commitments ("I'm actively working towards this, here's what I'm doing")
- Regular check-ins on progress rather than fixed dates
- Acknowledge both realities without forcing a resolution

### 4. Practical Plan
- What needs to happen before moving in? (financial stability, flat found, lease signed)
- What's realistic given current situation?
- What's the first concrete step?
