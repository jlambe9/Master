## Win State

A complete table: every financial obligation, amount, due date, status. No surprises.

## What to Audit

### Medical
- All outstanding medical bills (list each)
- Payment plans — what's the schedule?
- Due dates — what's overdue, what's upcoming?
- ADHD assessment/medication costs (private?)
- Any other health-related costs

### Housing Transition
- Current rent amount + end date
- New flat rent amount + start date
- Overlap period (double rent) — how many months?
- Deposit amount + when due
- Agency/admin fees
- Moving costs

### Debts / Obligations
- Credit cards
- Loans
- Overdraft
- Tax (HMRC — anything owed?)
- Company obligations

### Income
- Current income (if any)
- Savings / buffer
- Available credit

## Output

```
| Obligation | Amount | Due Date | Status | Payment Plan |
```

Plus: total monthly outgoings, total one-off costs over next 3 months, current cash position.
