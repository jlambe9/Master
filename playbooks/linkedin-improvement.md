## Global Levers (Ranked by Compound Impact)

### 🔴 HIGHEST
- **ICP Targeting Precision** — affects accept, intro reply, close. Moving from "founders" to "ADHD founders in tech, 50–200 employees, post-Series A" changes every number. Effort: 2–4 hrs.
- **Speed to Lead** — within 1hr vs 24hr drops reply rate ~50%. Requires automation. Effort: tool setup.

### 🟡 HIGH
- **Personalisation System** — 3 hooks per prospect (post, role, company). Template structure, personalise variables. Effort: design once, 2min/prospect.
- **Content Engine (3×/week)** — warms prospects passively via feed. Compounds over 60–90 days. Effort: 3–4 hrs/week.
- **Offer Crystallisation** — "6-week Brain Battery for ADHD founders — 83% improved focus by week 3" > "coaching." Effort: half-day.

### 🔵 MEDIUM
- **Confirmation sequence** — saves no-shows. Effort: 1hr automation.
- **Call script** — 60/20/20 diagnosis structure. Effort: half-day + ongoing.
- **Landing page** — prospects check before booking. Effort: 1–2 days.
- **A/B testing intros** — 3 variants, 50 each, pick winner. +40% conversations. Effort: 1 week/cycle.
