## What's Needed for £3,000/month

£3K = 6 clients = 1.5/week = 0.21/day closed

Working backwards from 35 CRs/day:
```
35 CRs → 15 accepts (42.9% ACTUAL)
     → 15 intros sent
     → 4.5 conversations needed/day
     → need 30% total conversation rate (vs 29% mid-case)
     → 1.1 calls booked/day (25% of convos)
     → 0.8 showed/day (70%)
     → 0.21 closed/day (27% close rate)
```

**Gap from mid-case to £3K: tiny.** Need to go from 29% → 30% conversation rate OR 25% → 27% close rate. Either is achievable with:
- Personalised FU1s (not templated)
- Speed to lead on intros (<1hr after accept)
- Structured sales call framework (diagnosis → prescription → offer)

## Maximum Revenue at 35 CRs/day

**Ceiling (optimistic case, all levers optimised):** £5,500/month
This requires hitting the high end of every benchmark: 34% conversation rate, 30% call booking, 80% show rate, 30% close rate. Aggressive but possible with excellent execution.

## £3K/month — Daily Non-Negotiables

These are the actions Jamie MUST do every day, working backwards from the £3K number:

### Morning Block (during cardio review, 15-20 min)
1. **Reply to all conversations** — these are your hottest leads (4-5 active/day at steady state)
2. **Review + send intros** — all 15 new accepts need intros same day
3. **Review + send FU1s** — personalised, different angle from intro (~11/day)
4. **Batch send FU2/FU3/breakups** — templated, 1 min (~23/day combined)

### Afternoon Block (30-60 min)
5. **Sales calls** — 1-2 per day at steady state (7-8/week)
6. **Post-call follow-up** — proposal/recap within 1hr of call
7. **Second reply pass** — catch afternoon responses
