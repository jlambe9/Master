# LinkedIn Daily Operations — Steady State Schedule

*~2.5–3.5 hrs/day total. Assumes 50 CRs/day, mid-case funnel.*

## Morning Block

| # | Activity | Time | Output |
|---|----------|------|--------|
| 1 | Review/edit AI intros, send | 15 min | 25 intros out |
| 2 | Review/edit AI FU1s, send | 10 min | 23 FU1s out |
| 3 | Glance FU2 queue, send | 3 min | 19 FU2s out |
| 4 | Send FU3s + breakups | 1 min | 35 msgs out |
| 5 | Reply to conversations | 20 min | 6–8 threads advanced |
| 6 | Queue tomorrow's 50 CRs | 5 min | 50 CRs queued |

## Afternoon Block

| # | Activity | Time | Output |
|---|----------|------|--------|
| 7 | Sales calls | 30–60 min | 1–2 calls/day |
| 8 | Post-call follow-up (proposal/recap/Calendly nudge) | 10 min | Per call |
| 9 | Second reply pass (catch afternoon replies) | 10 min | 2–3 threads |
| 10 | Onboard new closed clients | 20–30 min | ~1 every 2–3 days |

## End of Day

| # | Activity | Time | Output |
|---|----------|------|--------|
| 11 | CRM tagging + pipeline update | 5 min | Feedback loop closed |
| 12 | Review no-shows / reschedule | 5 min | Recover ~30% of no-shows |
| 13 | Content creation/scheduling | 20–30 min | 1 post drafted (3×/week) |

## Weekly Steady-State Output

| Metric | Volume |
|--------|--------|
| Messages sent | ~710 |
| Conversations active | 44 |
| Calls booked | 11 |
| Calls showed | 8 |
| Closed | 2 |
| Clients onboarded | 2 |
| Content posted | 3 |

## What's Automated (No Jamie Time)

- AI generates intros + FU1s overnight from profile data
- AI generates semi-templated FU2s
- FU3s + breakups fully templated, auto-queued
- Passive touches (profile views, post likes) automated via Expandi
- Confirmation/reminder sequence fires on booked calls
